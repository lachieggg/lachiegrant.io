How to fit and be "normal" in modern society:

  • Don't be poor
  • Don't act on your creative impulses or intellectual curiosity
Oh and don't piss off anybody too important.

Did I mention don't be poor?

90% of what people mean when they talk about "being normal" is basically about not being poor.

There are only some things worth sacrificing for the sake of appearances.

I think it's pretty sad that we live in a culture that is as superficial as ours seems to be.

Programming is something that is by a lot of people typically considered weird, until people realize how well it pays.

Which is sad, because I've always thought it was cool, regardless of what it pays.

Now so many people also think programming is cool, but just because of the salary.

People should just pursue what they love. Thankfully what I love pays well, many people aren't so lucky.

For me programming is and always will be cool.
