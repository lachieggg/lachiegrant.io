Australia's central bank has issued caution over heavy investments in cryptocurrency, flagging its long-term use as highly sceptical.

It's the same thing that hedge funds would have said about investing in GameStop.

Extreme caution is necessary when investing in the stock market! Only the experts know how to do it! It's very dangerous! You could lose all your money! Stay away!

Which is basically another way of saying, "Go away this is our game, retail investors are not allowed to make money the way we do."
